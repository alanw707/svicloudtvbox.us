Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is shipping?

We ship from the USA. Handling is 1–3 business days, with carrier ETAs shown at checkout.

Is there a warranty?

Yes. 1‑Year U.S. warranty against defects. See our Warranty/Returns page for details.

Does it require a monthly fee?

No monthly fees for the device. Third‑party apps may have their own subscriptions.

How do I set it up?

Plug into HDMI and power, connect Wi‑Fi, and follow on‑screen prompts. See our 3‑minute setup guide.

Is there Chinese language support?

Yes. The site and support are bilingual (English/中文). Toggle the language at the top of the page.

What’s the difference between 10P+ and 10S?

10P+ has 4GB/64GB and Kids/Karaoke apps; 10S is value‑focused with 2GB/32GB. See the compare page.
